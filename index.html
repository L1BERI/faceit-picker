Welcome to Faceit EGO Enhancer
(POSPICKER)!

Our extension currently has 2 main functions:
Position picker üìç
Players can write the positions they play on different maps and the extension will automatically send them to the chat immediately after choosing a map.
Comments for players üí¨
Players can write comments (notes) for any player in the lobby.
Then in the next matches, if they get matched with the player they left a comment for, they will know whether it is good or not.

How to use? ü§î

Using the extension is quite simple.
In order to enter the required positions on the maps, you need to go to the "Positions" tab, mark the cards and write the positions and click the "Save" button.
To leave comments, you need to click on "üí¨" next to the player's nickname, a modal window will appear where you need to enter a comment and click the "Save" button.
  1. Click on "üí¨" next to the player's nickname in the lobby.
  2. A modal window will appear. Enter your comment (note) about the player.
  3. Click the "Save" button to save your comment.
  4. Next time you meet this player, your comment will be shown automatically.
Comments have some settings:
  • Search players by nickname
  • Delete one comment
  • Disable comments
  • Remove all comments
